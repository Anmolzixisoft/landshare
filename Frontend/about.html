Our Story

Hailing from New Delhi, our expertise in transacting residential and commercial properties provided us with a distinct advantage in identifying growth zones for land acquisition and investment. At the time, our curiosity and experience led us to recognise the potential for lucrative land investment opportunities at attractive prices in the thriving metropolis, marking the beginning of our journey as land transaction advisors in Bangalore. We witnessed a pressing need for an organised way of land acquisition and advisory services.

After dedicating considerable time to learning, policy research, and building a network of trustworthy local partners for land sourcing, legal verification, and liaising, we executed a number of transactions, which helped us grow our expertise in transacting land. Through hard work and determination, we accomplished our goals, pushing our organisation to thrive.

The obstacles we overcame as we moved forward enabled us to progress and take on new challenges. With experience, we streamlined our process to invest in lands. With our success, we could see our vision of creating an organised way of transacting land becoming a reality.

Today, we are one of the leading Land Investment & Advisory Companies headquartered in Bangalore, the technology capital of India enabling investments for retail and corporate customers.

For our retail customers, we have curated customised land investment products under our parent organisation “Agrocorp” and enabled them to add to their portfolio land as a lifestyle investment.

For our corporate customers, we lead acquisitions for their Residential, Commercial and Industrial developments. With a footprint in Delhi NCR, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka & Goa, we undertake acquisitions with a strong focus on timely delivery and organised process.
